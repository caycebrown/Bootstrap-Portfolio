About Me

Hello, my name is Cayce and I am a beginning web developer studying full-stack web development at the University of Texas Coding Bootcamp! One of my favorite things about web development is the design/UI side of it. Getting a chance to bring innovative and engaging web applications to life is something that I look forward too! As a logic-lover and problem solver, programming and development for me is a rewarding activity. If you think so too, then let me know, and lets make something cool together!

When I'm not coding or researching development technologies you will find me outdoors. Sunshine, laketime, and anything involving a hike is A+ life material for me. I also enjoy just taking in the simple moments with my wife and daughter - who is already growing at an alarming rate. Thanks for checking out my portfolio!
